This is a recipe for chicken-tikka. Here are the instructions to make checken tikka
1) Take a 4 pounds of boneless skin less chicken
2) Cut vegetables like pepers, tomato, cauliflower and onions
3) Fry onions in until golden brown
4) Add all the vegetables and saute them
5) Add chicken and masala and saute them until chicken is almost done 
6) Add tomato and wait for it.
